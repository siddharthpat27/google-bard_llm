Kung Pao Chicken

Spicy stir-fried chicken with peanuts, vegetables, and chili peppers.

**Ingredients:** Chicken breast, peanuts, bell peppers, chili peppers, scallions, soy sauce, rice vinegar, Shaoxing wine, cornstarch, sugar

**Spice Level:** Medium

Mapo Tofu

Silken tofu in a spicy and savory sauce with ground pork and fermented black beans.

**Ingredients:** Silken tofu, ground pork, fermented black beans, chili bean paste, scallions, ginger, garlic, Sichuan peppercorns, soy sauce, rice vinegar, cornstarch

**Spice Level:** Spicy

Dan Dan Noodles

Spicy sesame noodles with a savory pork sauce and chili oil.

**Ingredients:** Noodles, ground pork, scallions, peanuts, sesame paste, chili oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar

**Spice Level:** Medium
